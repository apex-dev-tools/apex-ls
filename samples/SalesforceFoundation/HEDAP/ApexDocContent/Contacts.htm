Contact Related Classes

HEDA Contacts functionality is made up of these components:

  • Do Not Contact
  • Preferred Contact
  • Primary Affiliations

The classes involved with Contact include:

  • CON_CannotDelete_TDTM - Stops a Contact from being deleted if it has any Affiliation, Program Enrollment or Course Enrollment children.
  • CON_DoNotContact_TDTM - Handles changes to the deceased and do not contact fields on Contact.
  • CON_Preferred_TDTM - Populates default email and phone fields according to user preferences.
  • CON_PrimaryAffls_TDTM - Creates Affiliations when the user manually populates any of the primary affiliation lookup fields.
