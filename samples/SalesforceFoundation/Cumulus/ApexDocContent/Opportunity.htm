Opportunity Classes

NPSP Opportunity functionality is made up of these components:

  • Opportunity Contact Roles
  • Matching Donations
  • Opportunity Naming
  • Partial Soft Credits

The classes involved with Opportunity Contact Roles include:

  • OPP_OpportunityContactRoles_TDTM
  • OPP_OpportunityContactRoles_TEST

The classes involved with Matching Donations include:

  • OPP_MatchingDonationsBTN_CTRL
  • OPP_MatchingDonationsBTN_TEST

The classes involved with Opportunity Naming include:

  • OPP_OpportunityNaming
  • OPP_OpportunityNaming_BATCH
  • OPP_OpportunityNaming_TDTM
  • OPP_OpportunityNaming_TEST
  • OPP_INaming
  • OPP_MatchingDonationsBTN_CTRL
  • OPP_MatchingDonationsBTN_TEST

The classes involved with Partial Soft Credits include:

  • RLLP_OppPartialSoftCreditRollup
  • RLLP_OppPartialSoftCreditRollup_TEST
  • PSC_ManageSoftCredits_CTRL
  • PSC_ManageSoftCredits_TEST
  • PSC_Opportunity_TDTM
  • PSC_PartialSoftCredit_TDTM
  • PSC_PartialSoftCredit_TEST
