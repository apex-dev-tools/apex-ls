Contacts Classes

NPSP Contacts functionality is made up of these components:

  • Contact Delete Override
  • Do Not Contact

The classes involved with Contact Delete Override include:

  • CON_DeleteContactOverride_CTRL
  • CON_DeleteContactOverride_TEST

The classes involved with Do Not Contact include:

  • CON_DoNotContact_TDTM
  • CON_DoNotContact_TEST
