Engagement Plan Classes

NPSP Engagement Plans functionality is made of the following components:

  • Engagement Plan Trigger Support
  • Engagement Plan Task Trigger Support
  • Task Trigger Support
  • Utility Classes

The classes involved with Engagement Plan trigger support include:

  • EP_EngagementPlans_TDTM
  • EP_EngagementPlans_TEST

The classes involved with Engagement Plan Task trigger support include:

  • EP_EngagementPlanTaskValidation_TDTM
  • EP_EngagementPlanTaskValidation_TEST

The classes involved with Task Trigger support include:

  • EP_TaskDependency_TDTM
  • EP_TaskDependency_TEST
  • EP_TaskRollup_TDTM
  • EP_TaskRollup_TEST

The utility classes that support the Engagement Plans functionality include:

  • EP_EngagementPlans_UTIL
  • EP_Task_UTIL
