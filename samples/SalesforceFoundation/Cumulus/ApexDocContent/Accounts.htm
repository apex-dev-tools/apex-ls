Account Related Classes

NPSP supports 4 distinct types of Accounts.

  • Organizational Accounts - these are normal Salesforce Accounts, which typically represent a business, organization, or government agency.
  • One-to-One Accounts - these are shadow Accounts where each corresponds one-to-one with a Contact record.
  • Bucket Individual Account - a special Account that all Contacts who are not associated with a Organizational Account, can use as their Account.
  • Household Accounts - these are Accounts whose Contacts are all members of the same Household

The Account Model that is specified in NPSP Settings dictates which of the non-Organizational Accounts are automatically created and used&npsp; when a Contact is created. One-to-One and Bucket Individual Accounts where introduced in early version of the NPSP. Household Accounts were  introduced in NPSP 3.0, and are recommended as the preferred Account Model.

  • ACCT_Accounts_TDTM - Trigger Handler on Accounts, that ensures various system fields are correctly set.
  • ACCT_HH_Accounts_TEST - Test code for Household Accounts
  • ACCT_IndividualAccounts_TDTM - Trigger Handler on Contacts, that manages creating the appropriate Account for Contacts, when they are created or updated. This handles all three Account Models (Individual Bucket, One-to-One, and Household Account).
  • ACCT_ViewOverride_CTRL - Account action override for the View command, to allow the NPSP to redirect url's to the One-to-One account back to the Contact.
  • ACCT_ViewOverride_TEST - Test code for the Account view override.
  • CAO_Constants - Constants and helper methods used with the Account Model.
