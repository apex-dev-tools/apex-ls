Rollup Classes

NPSP Rollup functionality is made up of these components:

  • Opportunity Rollup Batch Classes
  • Opportunity Rollup Triggers and Utilities

The classes involved with Opportunity Rollup Batch Classes include:

  • RLLP_OppAccRollup_BATCH
  • RLLP_OppContactRollup_BATCH
  • RLLP_OppHouseholdRollup_BATCH
  • RLLP_OppSoftCreditRollup_BATCH
  • RLLP_OppRollupBATCH_TEST

The classes involved with Opportunity Rollup Triggers and Utilities include:

  • RLLP_OppRollup
  • RLLP_OppRollup_TDTM
  • RLLP_OppRollup_TEST
  • RLLP_OppRollup_TEST2
  • RLLP_OppRollup_UTIL
